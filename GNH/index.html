Gamma Nu Eta

GNH

Gamma Nu Eta is the National Information Technology Honor Society, that recognizes and promote students in the excellent study and leaderhip in Information Technolgy. Not only does this organization honor its memebers, it also fosters scholarship, leadership, and community involvemnt. To be apart of this society students need to have a strong academic performance, demonstrate leadership, show campus involvemnt, and have excellent character.
